NEW GAMEPLAY

We are really excited to give you the new gameplay of Catena. Thanks to your feedbacks, suggestions and curiosity about the game, we have finally brought Catena’s gameplay closer to a perfection.

※

The concept and basic gameplay is the same. You still need to come up with a new word with the ending letters of your opponent’s last word.

※

We have changed how you win or lose the game and we will be showing you the score you get with your game play.

To win the game you need to fill the bar with your colour. Each letter you use from your opponent’s word gives you a bar point.

SCORES & LEADERBOARDS

Now you also have a game score that will be carried to a leaderboard. Catena now has two leaderboards for both Single Player & Multi Player game, after finishing a game, your score will be added to your current leaderboard score.

To get higher game scores, first use as many letters as you can use from your opponent’s word, then make the word as long as you can.

MESSAGING

Yes, now you can attach a tiny message to your word to send your opponent. We pretty are sure you will behave.

NEW MATCH RESULT SCREEN

We also redesigned the match result screen to give you detailed information about your score and how you got it.
